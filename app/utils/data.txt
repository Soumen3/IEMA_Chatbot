PRODUCT CATEGORIES
=========================

STEM KITS: Practical learning kits for science and engineering concepts using hands-on assembly. Suitable for students and early learners. Examples include Solar Robotics Kits, DIY Motor Kits, Wind Car Kits, Magnetic Science Kits and more.

SCIENCE KITS: Simple educational kits to demonstrate real scientific principles such as solar energy, electric circuits, mechanical power, magnetism and physics fundamentals.

ROBOTICS KITS: Mechanical and electrical robotic kits with motors, gears, sensors, chassis and controllers. Kits help students build functioning robotic prototypes that demonstrate movement, power, automation and design.
